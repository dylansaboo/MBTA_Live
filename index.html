Welcome to MBTA.io

This web application allows for real time tracking of the MBTA trains. Please use our search box to find the train station you would like to receive live updates on, also please allow for location services as this allows us to show you the approximate walking distance to the train station you requested.

Current Known Bugs

As this is a work in progress there are a few known bugs that I will be resolving very soon. The biggest being end of lines do not display the correct train information currently as it just shows the next trains coming into the station.
